Manga Review

Destroy all of humanity. It can't be regenerated (Ch. 10)

I read the oneshot of this manga, and I absolutely loved it. I read all the translated chapters so far, and this manga is going in a great direction. The author is wasting no time with the story and instead of using Magic: The Gathering as a crutch, the author is going above and beyond in adding drama in a genre where I thought it would be impossible. Not to mention that it is a romantic-comedy manga, and I am a sucker for those.

Kaguya-sama (Ch. 163)

Kaguya-sama so far has been terrific. This latest chapter is hilarious. Fujiwara pretending to be bald to try to prank Kaguya was sooo funny for some reason. I also thought that Iino's reaction to Fujiwara pretending to be Ishigami's girlfriend was also funny. Not to mention Iino's nonchalant attitude about domestic abuse is still funny after about 25 chapters.
